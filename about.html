Hello. Vlad is a Front-End Developer & Designer based in Southampton, UK

With over 5 years experience as a Graphic Designer and over 3 years as a Front-Web Developer, I can confidently say that my current goal is to inspire people to embrace their passion and make a living out of it.

Being involved in a lot of real-world projects, such as creating and selling websites concepts, leading teams in resolving design based issues, or using freelancing as a way of getting paid on what I like to do in my spare time, I consired myself a proficient when it comes to understanding and using Front-Web Technologyes (HTML, CSS/Sass, JS/JQuery, Bootstrap, Gulp, Jekyll, Git & Github) or creating almost anything using Adobe CC programs (Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Premiere).
