About

"...just me, myself and I"

Vlad is a Front-End Developer & Designer based in Southampton, UK

I started 5 years ago as a Graphic Designer doing website mockups, personal branding on social media and flyers for different local events. Then I taught myself how to Code in order for my designs to have a real impact as actual web applications.

In the present, I am a passionate Front-End Developer using and learning constantly the latest web technologies. I am a perfectionist and I love challenges, the harder the milestone, the more I enjoy it. Always looking for project oportunities and trying to inspire people to embrace their passion.
